Level 3: Secure Browsing & Wi-Fi

Your final mission: Learn to spot secure connections and safe Wi-Fi networks to keep your data private in public places.

Secure Websites (HTTPS)

When you visit a website, always look at the address bar. A secure website starts with HTTPS and shows a padlock icon. This means your connection to the site is encrypted.

Safe Wi-Fi Networks

Public Wi-Fi can be risky. Always prefer networks that require a password. Avoid "Open" or "Free Public Wi-Fi" for sensitive activities like banking or shopping.
